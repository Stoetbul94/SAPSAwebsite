PLACE YOUR SPONSOR LOGO FILES HERE

Copy your sponsor logo image files into this directory.

Recommended naming:
- sponsor-company-name.png
- sponsor-brand-name.png
- sponsor-partner-name.png

Examples:
- sponsor-acme-corp.png
- sponsor-firearms-supplier.png
- sponsor-range-partner.png

After copying, reference them in code as:
- /sponsors/sponsor-company-name.png
- /sponsors/sponsor-brand-name.png
etc.
