PLACE YOUR SAPSA LOGO FILES HERE

Copy your SAPSA logo image files into this directory.

Recommended filenames:
- sapsa-logo.png (or .svg, .jpeg)
- sapsa-icon.png (for smaller icon version)
- sapsa-logo-white.png (for dark backgrounds)

After copying, reference them in code as:
- /logo/sapsa-logo.png
- /logo/sapsa-icon.png
etc.
